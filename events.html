10th Criminology and Criminal Justice Conference, 2025

Hosted by the Department of Sociology and Criminal Justice

About this Event

The Criminology and Criminal Justice Conference (CCJC) is a creation of the Department of Sociology and Criminal Justice at the United States International University-Africa in Nairobi, Kenya. The CCJC's mission is to provide a platform for discourse on contemporary issues in criminology and criminal justice.

This year's conference will focus on emerging trends in cybersecurity, transnational crime, and restorative justice. We welcome submissions from academics, practitioners, and postgraduate students.

Important Dates:

  • Call for Papers: March 8, 2025
  • Abstract Submission Deadline: May 19, 2025
  • Submission of Full Papers: June 25, 2025
