Lady Justice Joyce Aluoch Intervarsity ADR Moot Competition

Hosted by the USIU-Africa Legal Studies Department

About this Event

The Lady Justice Joyce Aluoch Intervarsity ADR Moot Competition was established to provide students with a transformative experience in Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR), focusing on mediation and negotiation. It is an initiative of the Centre for Alternative Dispute Resolution and the International Law Students Association (ILSA) USIU-Africa Chapter.

This competition aims to equip future legal professionals with the essential skills of diplomacy, critical thinking, and peaceful conflict resolution. Participants will engage in simulated legal disputes, honing their abilities to mediate and negotiate outcomes, which are vital in today's global legal landscape.

Key Objectives:

  • To promote the use of ADR mechanisms in resolving disputes.
  • To provide a platform for law students to practice and enhance their advocacy skills.
  • To foster networking among students, academics, and legal practitioners.
