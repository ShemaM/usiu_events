About The Business Club

The Business Club aims to fully develop the potential of its members as future professionals in the business world through mentorship and training by reputable companies. We provide a platform for students to engage with business and entrepreneurship concepts outside the classroom.

Our activities include case competitions, workshops with industry leaders, company visits, and networking events. We are committed to fostering an entrepreneurial spirit and providing practical skills that complement academic learning.
